Studio Myo

Studio Myo is a dynamic digital studio focused on innovation, quality, efficiency, and adaptability. We strive to create fresh, engaging experiences that push the boundaries of design and technology, always ready to evolve with the needs of our clients.

The Challange

The challenge was to create and brand ourselves as a full digital studio, ready to engage with real-world clients. We were tasked with developing a unique studio name, defining the type of studio we wanted to be, and building a cohesive brand identity that would represent our values and skills in the digital space.

To Start

We initially set up a Trello board to split tasks among group members, but after receiving feedback from our coaches, we switched to Jira, recognizing it as a more professional tool for task management. We then created a Team Contract to discuss our strengths and weaknesses, as well as establish rules for attendance and communication, ensuring that we worked in a professional and organized manner.


I then began defining the brand's core values to create a strong foundation for the design elements, ensuring they aligned with the brandâ€™s identity and message.

Design

After defining the core values, I began designing the logo. My goal was to create a modern, minimalist design that was both impactful and more creative than a simple text-based logo. I drew inspiration from Pinterest, exploring the use of positive and negative space. To organize my ideas, I compiled my inspirations and color palette into a stylescape to present to my group.


After receiving feedback and reviewing my team members' designs, I began exploring new logo ideas, focusing on the core value of 'adaptability' and conveying a sense of 'flexibility'. Drawing inspiration from the logos in my stylescape, I experimented with negative space to create more dynamic and visually engaging designs. To refine my concepts and enhance my proficiency with professional tools, I developed these logos using Adobe Illustrator.
