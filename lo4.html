Stakeholder Involvement

STUDIO KROM


To maintain a professional relationship with our client, Studio Krom, we held biweekly check-ins to keep them informed about our progress and gather feedback. I took on the communication role within our team, serving as the main point of contact with Joris from Krom. This involved managing scheduling, sharing updates, and coordinating meetings through WhatsApp and email. PROJECT COACHES


In addition to our client meetings, we also had regular check ins with our project coaches in order to get feedback as a group but also on an individual basis, in between portfolio reviews. This feedback was always regularly logged into Feedpulse in order to keep track of our progress and refer back to when needed.


Team Comunication

At the start of the project, we used Trello to divide tasks and track progress. However, after receiving feedback from our coaches, we recognized the need for a more structured and professional tool, so we transitioned to Jira. This allowed us to better align with industry standards for digital project management, offering more advanced features for task assignment, sprint planning, and backlog organization. Alongside this, we created a Team Charter to establish a shared understanding of our goals, working styles, communication expectations, and responsibilities. These tools and agreements helped us maintain transparency, accountability, and effective collaboration throughout the project.
